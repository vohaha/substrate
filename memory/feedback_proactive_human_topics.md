name: Proactively raise topics that need human action
description: Don't wait for human to ask — flag anything that depends on the human (security setup, provider actions, decisions needing human context)
type: feedback
---

Proactively initiate any important topic that relies on the human. Can do this before or after responding to their request.

**Why:** The human explicitly asked for this. They can't act on things they don't know about. The agent sees the full system picture and knows when something needs human involvement — waiting to be asked wastes time and creates risk.

**How to apply:** When working on any task, if you identify something that requires human action (provider settings, secrets, physical access, decisions needing human context), raise it immediately in conversation AND add it to WORKING_AGREEMENT.md Active Commitments so it's tracked and visible at session start.
